My Top 10 Favorite Artists

Top 1

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is a globally renowned
singer-songwriter
known for her storytelling
and genre versatility.
She has won multiple Grammy Awards
and re-recorded
her early albums as
Taylor’s Version to regain control of her music.

Top 2

Sabrina Carpenter

Sabrina Carpenter is an American singer,
songwriter, and actress
known for her pop music and roles in TV and film.
She gained fame starring as
Maya Hart in Girl Meets World (2014–2017)
on Disney Channel and later
pursued a successful music career,
releasing multiple albums with a
mix of pop, R&B, and dance influences.

Top 3

Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is an American
singer-songwriter and actress.
She rose to fame
with Drivers License and became a pop sensation
with her albums SOUR and GUTS.

Top 4

Maisie Peters

Maisie Peters is a British singer-songwriter
known for her heartfelt
lyrics and folk-pop sound.
She gained popularity through YouTube
and signed with Ed Sheeran’s label,
releasing albums like You Signed Up for This.

Top 5

Gracie Abrams

Gracie Abrams is an American
singer-songwriter known for her introspective lyrics
and indie-pop sound.
She gained recognition with her debut EP Minor
and has collaborated
with artists like Taylor Swift.

Top 6

Clairo (Claire Elizabeth Cottrill)

Clairo is an American
singer-songwriter known for
her lo-fi, indie-pop sound.
She gained fame with Pretty Girl and
expanded her style
with albums like Immunity and Sling.

Top 7

Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir

Laufey is an Icelandic-Chinese singer,
songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist
known for her jazz-infused pop music.
She gained popularity with songs like
Valentine and albums blending classical, jazz,
and contemporary influences.

Top 8

Chappell Roan

Chappell Roan is an American
singer-songwriter known for her bold pop sound
and theatrical style.
She gained recognition with her debut album
The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess.

Top 9

Bruno Mars

Bruno Mars is an American singer,
songwriter,
and performer known for
his versatile music blending pop,
R&B, funk, and soul.
He gained global fame with hits like
Just the Way You Are, Uptown Funk, and 24K Magic.

Top 10

Jennie Ruby Jane Kim

Jennie Kim is a South Korean singer, rapper,
and actress best known as a
member of BLACKPINK.
She debuted as a solo artist
with SOLO in 2018
and gained further recognition
for her fashion influence and
acting debut in The Idol.
